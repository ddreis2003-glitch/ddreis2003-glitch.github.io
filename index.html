01 — Profile

About

I graduated from the University of Arizona's Eller College of Management with a degree in accounting, and I'm joining CliftonLarsonAllen's deal services practice in Boston this fall. My internships and campus leadership roles gave me a practical grounding in how finance teams actually operate — from federal and state tax returns to event-day financial coordination for a live sporting event.

I'm equally interested in the intersection of finance and technology. I spend my free time building AI projects that automate financial workflows and surface insights from data, aiming to bring the same rigor I apply to reconciliations and tax research to the tools I build.

I'm especially interested in the institutional capital moving into professional sports — the investors buying into teams and leagues, and how that is resetting what a franchise is worth. Sports have been a constant in my life, whether playing or watching, and the shift in who ends up owning them is the direction I want to build toward.
